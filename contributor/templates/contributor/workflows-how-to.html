TURING Algorithms for Computation with Finite Dynamical Systems

  1. How-to Guide

A workflow is a series of computations or data manipulation steps.
TURING allows you to create a workflow from the algorithms published here. TURING utilizes AlgoPiper, a web-based graphical tool to build and execute workflows.

To start-off,

  1. Click the launch button below and walk through the interactive tutorial.
  2. Create your workflows.
  3. Export your workflows using the top-right menu.
  4. Publish your workflow here by submitting the exported JSON (copy and paste).
